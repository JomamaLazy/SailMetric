Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

We stripped away the bloated features of yacht-racing computers to focus entirely on the metrics that actually make dinghy sailors faster.

Maneuver Speed Analytics

Quantify exactly how much speed you lose through a tack or gybe. Instantly know if a maneuver was a net-gain or a net-loss.

Mechanical Heel Tracking

Built-in IMUs measure your boat's heel angle in real-time. Keep the boat flat, maximize the waterline, and visually track stability.

Tactical Compass Shifts

Automated lift and header detection. Lock in a baseline True Wind Direction and let the display tell you when the wind shifts.
